Rental Management System — DNSC

A web-based information system designed to manage the rental operations of Davao del Norte State College. It enables administrators to efficiently handle rental requests, monitor room or facility availability, manage tenant records, process payments, and generate reports in a centralized platform. The system helps streamline the management of rentable facilities such as hostel rooms, function halls, equipment, and other institutional resources — reducing manual paperwork, improving record accuracy, and enhancing transaction transparency.
